HARMONY PARK is a stylish and modern residential complex that is in harmony with nature and gives a feeling of calmness and comfort in the dynamic everyday life of a person in the big city.

Harmony Park is a gated residential complex and consists of a total of 75 apartments, of which 30 two-room, 25 three-room and 20 four-room apartments.

Residents of the complex can enjoy a summer cinema, which is located on the roof of the building.

The complex has 24-hour reception, concierge, physical security and a specially designed security system.
